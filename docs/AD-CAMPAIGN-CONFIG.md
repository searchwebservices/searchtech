## NOTES FOR SETUP

1. **Start with Exact Match keywords** - They're more expensive per click but highly targeted
2. **Don't optimize for conversions immediately** - Let the algorithms learn for 2 weeks
3. **Monitor search terms daily** the first week - Add negative keywords aggressively
4. **The goal is brand awareness + organic boost** - Don't stress about direct conversions yet
5. **Consistency matters more than budget** - $5/day for 6 months beats $30/day for 1 month
